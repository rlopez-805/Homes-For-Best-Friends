Best Friends Shelter

Providing every pet the home they deserve.

Everyone deserves a Best Friend

Best Friends Shelter is Los Angeles-based organization that works to keep animals off the streets and in cozy homes. Adopt Now!

Services

Our organization offers the following services:

Animal Rescue

We rescue stray animals from being in the unsafe streets and treat any injuries they might have.

Animal Placement

Rescued animals are provided a safe home until a permanent adoptive home is found.

Animal Protection

We strive to protect animals in the way of harm and prevent any further harm done by humans

Get Involved

Come to our location for opportunities to volunteer as an instructor, participate in workshops, or even adopt!
