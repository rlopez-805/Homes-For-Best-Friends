Achievements

BFS has rescued more than 100+ pets that have been left on the streets, neglected,injured, alone, and hungry. All the pets we help were treated with love and care are now in safe and loving environments. It is our goal to support pets in finding an atmosphere where they feel loved and safe and to be able to find them a loving and safe home with people who make them feel cared for and most of all loved.
